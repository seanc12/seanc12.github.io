64. 10 Years Micellenea

‘Miscellanea’ is the first taste of new music off the upcoming ‘From Birth to Burial’ album, which arrives April 21.

63. P.O.D. This Goes Out To You

P.O.D., still going strong in their 23rd year as a band, are back with a new single from their upcoming tenth album The Awakening. For this album, the group reunited with Howard Benson, the producer who helmed their 1999 breakthrough The Fundamental Elements of Southtown.

62. Seether Save Today

HardDrive Radio has premiered SEETHER's music video for the song "Save Today". Check it out below. The track is taken from SEETHER's latest album, "Isolate And Medicate", landed at No. 4 on the Billboard album chart last year. The follow-up to 2011's "Holding Onto Strings Better Left To Fray" marked the group's debut on Concord Record Group after spending the first decade of their career with Wind-Up Records.

61. Asking Alexandria I Wont Give In

"I Won't Give In" is the first single from the upcoming Asking Alexandria album. The single was released May 26, 2015. It is the first song released with new singer Denis Stoff, after the departure of former vocalist Danny Worsnop in January 2015.

60. AC/DC Rock Or Bust

Rock or Bust is the seventeenth studio album by Australian hard rock band AC/DC. It is their fifteenth internationally released studio album and the sixteenth to be released in Australia. It is the shortest studio album ever released by the band; at approximately 35 minutes, it is two minutes shorter than their previous shortest album, Flick of the Switch, which was released in 1983. Rock or Bust also was the group's first album without founding member Malcolm Young, a rhythm guitarist and songwriter who had to exit the band in 2014 due to health concerns.

59. Devour The Day Faith

Devour the Day is an American hard rock band founded by former Egypt Central members Joey "Chicago" Walser and Blake Allison in 2012.

58. Awolnation Hollow Moon

"Hollow Moon (Bad Wolf)" is a song by American electronic rock band Awolnation. It was released as the lead single from their second studio album Run (2015). The song became their first number-one hit on the Billboard Alternative Songs chart in early 2015.

57. Theory Of A Deadman Blow

Corey Taylor may have penned a book called You’re Making Me Hate You about his gripes with today’s society, but Theory of a Deadman have managed to condense their dislikes all into one song and hilarious video called “Blow.”

56. Sevendust Thank You

To sweeten the pot a bit, the band have released their first single, and it sounds like something rock radio stations around the country will be throwing in heavy rotation for the months to come. The track is called "Thank You" and if you're a fan of Sevendust, you will enjoy it. If not, this probably isn't going to change your mind.

55. Royal Blood Figure It Out

"Figure It Out" is a song written by Mike Kerr and Ben Thatcher of British rock duo Royal Blood. The song was originally recorded by the duo for the band's eponymous debut studio album, Royal Blood, where it appears as the third track on the album. The track also appeared on the band's fourth single, released by Black Mammoth Records and Warner Bros. Records on 18 August 2014 as the lead single promoting Royal Blood. "Figure It Out" also appears on the soundtrack for Pro Evolution Soccer 2016.

54. Highly Suspect Bloodfeather

Highly Suspect is a rock music trio who got their start in 2009 by playing cover sets in Cape Cod, Massachusetts. The band was founded by twins Rich and Ryan Meyer, their cousin Mike Meyer, and best friend, Johnny Stevens.

53. Pop Evil Ways To Get High

Get ready for something almost as eye catching as it is ear catching from Pop Evil. The band has just released one of the catchiest songs of their career with the single “Ways to Get High” and they turned to their longtime directing collaborator Johan Carlen for the song’s video.

52. Highly Suspect Lydia

"Lydia" is a song by American rock band Highly Suspect. It was released as the lead single from their debut album Mister Asylum (2015). The song hit the top 5 on the Billboard Mainstream Rock chart in August 2015.

51. Godsmack 1000 HP

Godsmack are moving one step closer to releasing their ‘1000hp’ album, as the group has just unveiled the track listing for the disc. The band has already released the title track for the album, and now fans can get a look at the titles for the other songs on the disc below.

50. Shinedown State Of My Head

"State of My Head" is a song by American hard rock band Shinedown. It was released in October 2015 as the second single from the band's fifth studio album, Threat to Survival.

49. Offspring Coming For You

"Coming for You" is a song by the American punk rock band The Offspring. It was premiered on Radio Contraband on January 30, 2015,[2][3][4] and posted on YouTube and made available for digital download the same day.[5] "Coming for You" is The Offspring's second official new release (following the 2014 covers EP Summer Nationals) since Days Go By (2012).[6] The single climbed on various Billboard rock charts, reaching No. 1 on Mainstream Rock (the band's first since "Gone Away" in 1997), top 20 on Alternative Songs and top 25 on overall Hot Rock Songs.

48. Hellyeah Moth

Hellyeah have unleashed the new single ‘Moth’ off their latest album, ‘Blood for Blood.’ The track is one of the standout cuts on the disc, and we asked about the tune when they recently stopped by our studio.

47. Pretty Reckless Messed Up World

It may be a messed up world that we live in, but The Pretty Reckless chose a day at the beach for the setting of their new video ‘Messed Up World (F’d Up World).’ That’s just one of the ironic touchtones of the clip that has just arrived online.

46. Rise Against I Dont Want To Be Here

"I Don't Want to Be Here Anymore" is a song by American punk rock band Rise Against. The song was released as the lead single from their seventh album, titled The Black Market on June 10, 2014, and was sent to radio the following day. It debuted at twenty two on the Alternative Songs chart and has become their highest charting song on the Mainstream Rock Songs chart peaking at number 5.

45. Starset Carnivore

Starset -- Carnivore. From the album TRANSMISSIONS

44. Black Label Society My Dying Time

My Dying time is a single by Black Label Society, from their first album since Nick Catanese's departure from the band. The single shows influence from numerous albums of Zakk's band, but mostly toward their last album Order of the Black, and was released with the cover and track list of its album. It was available with the pre-order of the album on iTunes on January 21, 2014.

43. Royal Blood Out Of The Black

"Out of the Black" is a song written by Mike Kerr and Ben Thatcher of British rock duo Royal Blood. The song was originally recorded by the duo for release as a single, appearing on the band's debut single of the same name, released by Black Mammoth Records on 11 November 2013. The track later appeared as the opening track on the band's debut extended play Out of the Black, and as the opening track on the band's eponymous debut studio album, Royal Blood. The track was also part of the soundtrack to the film Who Am I – No System Is Safe, as well as the video games Forza Horizon 2 and EA Sports UFC. It was also used in a featurette for the Netflix series Daredevil at the 2015 New York Comic Con.

42. In This Moment Sick Like Me

"Sick Like Me" is a song by American band In This Moment. It is the first single taken from the band's fifth album Black Widow.

41. Three Days Grace Painkiller

"Painkiller" is a song by Canadian rock band Three Days Grace. This is the first single released by Three Days Grace with new singer Matt Walst, following the departure of Adam Gontier. It is the lead single off the band's fifth studio album Human.

40. Pretty Reckless Follow Me Down

This song is performed by The Pretty Reckless and appears on the album Going To Hell (2014).

39. Starset Halo

Track 3 of the long awaited TRANSMISSIONS album from Starset.

38. Ghost Cirice

“Cirice” is the first piece of new music fans are getting from the upcoming Meliora album.

37. All That Remains This Probably Wont End Well

"This Probably Won't End Well" is the first official single song by American metal band All That Remains from their seventh album, The Order of Things. It will be released January 13, 2015.

36. Foo Fighters Outside

"Outside" is a song by American rock band Foo Fighters that was released as the fifth single from their eighth studio album, Sonic Highways in August 2015. The song marked a 30th appearance by the Foo Fighters on Billboard's Alternative Songs chart tying them with Green Day and Red Hot Chili Peppers for third-most appearances.

Hellyeah address domestic violence in the powerful new lyric video for Hush.

34. Chevelle Take Out The Gunman

"Take Out the Gunman" is the lead single from Chevelle's seventh studio album, La Gárgola. The song debuted on February 3, 2014, via the band's Vevo and YouTube channel, with the single being released the next day.
